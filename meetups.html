Bold Art Pop-Up Gallery

Join us for Bold Art's Monthly Pop-Up Gallery at Alchemist! A themed art experience featuring a stunning collection of curated art pieces, AR interactions, and a chance to meet the artists. Discover creativity, interact with augmented reality, and take art home! Enjoy live demos, vibrant conversations, and an electric atmosphere that brings art to life. Whether you’re an art enthusiast or just looking for a fun and inspiring evening, this is the place to be. Don’t miss out on this unforgettable celebration of creativity and innovation!

Location: Alchemist, Nairobi

Date: Every First Saturday of the Month
